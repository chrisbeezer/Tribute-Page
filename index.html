Scion Tribute Page

A Division from Toyota

A timeline of historical events:

  • January 2003 - The xA and xB are reveiled at the Greater Los Angeles Auto Show
  • June 2003 - Scion launches at 105 Toyota dealerships in California
  • January 2004 - Scion debuts the tC at the North American International Auto Show
  • February 2004 - Scion rolls out South, Southeast, and East Coast Toyota dealerships in the US
  • June 2004 - Scion vehicles available nationwide and begins selling the tC
  • December 2006 - Scion holds an invitation only, no camera event to debut the next generation xB and the new xD which would become the successor to the xA
  • February 2007 - The 2nd generation xB and the new xD are publicly reveiled at the Chicago Auto Show
  • September 2010 - Scion expands to Canada dealerships starting in Montreal, Toronto, and Vancouver
  • 2015 - Scion releases two new vehicles the iA sedan and iM wagon; the iQ, xB, and xD are discontinued
  • February 2016 - Toyota announces that the Scion brand would be phased out at the end of the year; the FR-S, iA, and iM would be rebranded as Toyota vehicles while the tC would be discontinued at the end of the year
